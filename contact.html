Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about our banking applications, loan schedules, and support configurations.

You can open an account digitally by filling out our online registration form above and uploading your credentials. Alternatively, you can visit any of our branch networks with a valid means of identity, recent passport photograph, and utility bill.
Standard loan applications require your valid government-issued ID, Bank Verification Number (BVN), National Identification Number (NIN), proof of income (such as 3-6 months bank statements), and an executed guarantor profile form depending on the facility class.
Personal or micro-business credit evaluation typically takes between 24 to 48 hours following full verification assessment and document ingestion. Structural asset financing or SME facility reviews may take up to 5 business days.
Our physical branch network operates Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Our web applications, digital banking dashboards, and automated transfer channels remain available 24/7.
You can reach our dedicated support desk by calling our toll-free phone metrics (+234 800 123 4567), emailing support@egomfb.com, or submitting a structural request ticket using the contact form configuration on this page.
Still have pending clarification requests? Our virtual assistant is always active to navigate live resolution workflows.
